One local man, who has certainly come a long way, is Oli Ullah, owner and executive chef at Kasturi Indian Restaurant in Lucan.

Oli moved to Ireland from Bangladesh in 1986, and he trained to be a chef, starting his career in Ireland in the Eastern Tandoori, which was located in South William Street. Oli spent nine years working there, before opening his first restaurant, Tulsi, on Lower Baggott Street in 1996. Since opening Kasturi in 2001 in Lucan Village, the restaurant has won the prestigious Bridgestone award for food excellence four times.
