Welcome to China Sichuan Restaurant

We are located on Ballymoss Road in the Sandyford Industrial Park in our new, modern, purpose built restaurant within 100 meters of the Stillorgan Luas stop. The restaurant seats approximately 90 people in relaxed and informal surroundings. We have a heated terrace area protected from the weather for dining or just a relaxed drink. The restaurant is open for lunch Mondays to Fridays from 12pm to 3pm and on Sundays from 12pm through to 9pm with our Sunday Menu. Dinner is served Monday to Saturday from 5pm to 10pm.
